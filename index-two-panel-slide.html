BAKÉLITE®

Polyoxybenzylmethylenglycolanhydride, or simply: BAKÉLITE®. In the early 1900s, Bakélite®, the first fully synthetic thermoset was reported by Belgian chemist Léo Baekeland. Since its discovery, it has had a myriad of applications including radios, phones, cameras, musical instruments, plugs and switches. Having all but disappeared in the manufacture of consumer goods today, NEW JUMO CONCEPT has nonetheless undertaken the task of preserving this material in its rerelease of the JUMO Classique, in order to stay true to the original’s quality and spirit.

The arm

The arm, composed of 2 brass parts, has been reinforced on its 3 rotational axes, in order to ensure the JUMO Classique ’s strength over time.
The switch is now activated on demand, and not by the opening / closing of the lamp.

+

THE BASE

The cable spool under the base of the lamp, which holds the cable by spring with space to insert the plug, demonstrates the evolution of the JUMO Classique.
